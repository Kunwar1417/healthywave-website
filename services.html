Skin and Beauty Treatments – Designed for Real Results

Every treatment we offer is medically approved, scientifically backed, and tailored to your unique skin condition. Dr. Aman Jeet Handa personally supervises each treatment plan to ensure safety, comfort, and results you can trust.

01

Dermabrasion

For: Acne scars, uneven skin texture, fine lines

A medical skin-resurfacing treatment that gently removes the outer layers of damaged skin, revealing fresher, smoother, and more even-toned skin underneath. Highly effective for scarring and signs of ageing.

02

Ultrasonic Therapy

For: Deep cleansing, dull skin, blackheads

Using high-frequency sound waves, this treatment penetrates deep into the skin to cleanse, exfoliate, and rejuvenate without irritation. Perfect for sensitive skin types.

03

Galvanics

For: Dehydrated skin, sagging skin, dull complexion

An ionic therapy that uses safe galvanic currents to enhance product absorption, hydrate the skin deeply, and improve overall skin tone and firmness.

04

HIFU

For: Skin tightening, anti-ageing, non-surgical face lift

A premium non-surgical lifting treatment. HIFU uses focused ultrasound energy to stimulate collagen production deep within the skin, lifting and tightening areas like the face, jawline, and neck – with no downtime.

05

Dermasonic

For: Pore cleansing, skin renewal, oily skin

Combines sonic vibrations with skin-care actives to clear clogged pores, remove dead cells, and renew the skin's surface – leaving it visibly smoother and brighter.

Hydrafacial

For: Instant glow, hydration, dull skin

A 3-in-1 treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and infuses the skin with intensive serums. The result is glowing, deeply hydrated skin – perfect before events or as a regular maintenance treatment.
