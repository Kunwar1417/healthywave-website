FAQ

Questions You Might Have

Everything you need to know before you book your first visit.

Is the first consultation free?+

Yes. We offer a complimentary first consultation with Dr. Aman Jeet Handa so she can assess your skin concern and recommend the right treatment – with no obligation to proceed.

Are the treatments safe?+

Absolutely. Every treatment offered at Healthy Wave is medically approved and performed under direct supervision of a qualified doctor. We do not use unproven procedures or products.

How many sessions will I need?+

This depends entirely on your skin condition and the treatment recommended. Dr. Handa will give you an honest assessment during your consultation, including a realistic timeline and expected results.

Do you treat hormonal skin issues like PCOD-related acne?+

Yes. Dr. Handa's medical background allows her to understand and treat skin issues that stem from hormonal conditions like PCOD and thyroid disorders – something purely cosmetic clinics often cannot do.

I am from outside Bareilly. Can I still visit?+

Yes. We regularly treat patients from Pilibhit, Shahjahanpur, Rampur, Moradabad, and other nearby cities. You can call us or fill the inquiry form and we will help you plan your visit.
